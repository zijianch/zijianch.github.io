Summary of my research

https://orcid.org/0000-0001-9640-6096

My research interest lies in computational neuroimaging. During my Master's, I was working in Prof. Moo K. Chung's group, where I used PDEs, topology and graph theories to develop methodologies of signal processing on manifolds and study the (1) anatomical structures of human brains, and (2) intrinsic geometry and connectivity pattern of the brain network.
