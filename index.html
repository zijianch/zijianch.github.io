Summary of my research

https://orcid.org/0000-0001-9640-6096

My research interest lies in computational neuroscience. I am currently focusing on brain network analysis. Utilizing differential geometry (specifically, spectral geometry) and graph theory, I study the intrinsic geometry of the brain network and its embedding into lower dimensional manifolds through high-resolution fMRI data.

Before that, as an undergraduate student, I did research in clinical trial design/analysis and statistical estimation algorithm under supervision of Prof. Jin Xu.
